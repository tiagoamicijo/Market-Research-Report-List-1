<p><p>The Voice Controller Market is categorized into two main types: Independent Control and Integrated Control. In contrast, Integrated Control systems are designed to work in conjunction with other devices or platforms, offering a unified control experience. Both types enhance user convenience, but Independent Control offers standalone functionality, while Integrated Control emphasizes connectivity and interoperability with broader smart home ecosystems.</p></p>
