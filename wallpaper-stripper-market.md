<p><ul><li>Electric Steam Stripper</li><li>Handle Wallpaper Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wallpaper stripper market consists of two main types: electric steam strippers and hand-held wallpaper strippers. Electric steam strippers utilize heated steam to loosen adhesive and make wallpaper removal easier and faster, making them ideal for larger projects. They often feature bigger water tanks and are designed for efficiency. In contrast, hand-held wallpaper strippers are manually operated tools that typically include scrapers or blades for peeling off wallpaper. They are portable, cost-effective, and suitable for smaller tasks or touch-ups.</p></p>
