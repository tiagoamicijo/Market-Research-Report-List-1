<p><p>The dry cleaning machines market can be categorized into two main types: single solvent and multi-solvent dry cleaning machines. Single solvent machines utilize a single type of solvent, typically perchloroethylene (Perc) or hydrocarbons, offering a straightforward cleaning approach. In contrast, multi-solvent machines are designed to operate with multiple solvents, allowing for greater versatility in fabric care and cleaning efficiency. This flexibility makes them suitable for diverse garments, catering to various customer needs and enhancing overall market appeal.</p></p>
