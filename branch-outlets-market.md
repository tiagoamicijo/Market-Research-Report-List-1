<p><strong>The Branch Outlets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded</li><li>Socket-Weld</li><li>Butt-Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Branch outlets in the piping market facilitate connections and fluid flow within systems. Threaded outlets feature a screw-like design for secure attachment, ideal for low-pressure applications. Socket-weld outlets involve welding the pipe end to a pre-deepened outlet for robust connections, suitable for higher-pressure environments. Butt-weld outlets are joined by welding the edges of pipes directly, ensuring a continuous flow path and maximum strength, often used in high-pressure or high-temperature applications. Each type serves specific needs based on pressure, size, and application compatibility.</p></p>
